What is Scamonator?
A website where people can report scam emails/email addresses and texts/phone numbers, and also to check if a number/email is a known scam email/number.
How to avoid Scammers
10 tips to avoid scams and scammers:
1. Do not open emails from people you don’t know and/or email addresses you don’t recognize.
2. Try to only answer phone numbers you recognize.
3. Don’t click on links and attachments in unexpected emails and unsecure websites.
4. Don’t reply to scam calls, emails, and texts.
5. Don’t click on any pop-ups that you don’t recognize.
6. Hang up on cold callers and ignore or block cold texts.
7. If someone calls you from a company, ask for their name, employee ID, and other relevant information.
8. Don’t use public, unsecure WIFI to make any financial transactions (online shopping or online banking).
9. Never send money to people you haven’t met in person.
10. Don’t give personal information to people your unsure about, whether while calling, texting, or emailing.
Mission Statement
The goal of our project, Scamonator, is to reduce the amount of people that are harmed by the increasing number of scams present in our technologically progressing society.
