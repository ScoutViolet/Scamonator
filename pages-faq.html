Basic Questions
1. What is a Scam?

A scam is a fraudulent business scheme that takes money, resources, and/or good from an unsuspecting person. They can be conducted through email, phone, and text.

2. How does Scamonator work?

Users can report phone numbers and email addresses through a Google Form on the website. Along with the address/phone number, they also need to report the suspicious content sent to then.

The data from the Google Form is automatically input into a Google Sheet. The phone number/email address and its cotnent are entered as a set.

Everyday, someone will manually download the data from the Google Sheet into a CSV File.Then, they will upload the CSV file onto the website.

Users can then search on the website for potential scam phone numbers and email addresses or the content in the message they received. A python program will then search through the CSV file and return the relevant/matching information.
